# Contributing to Awesome Transnational Criminal Audits by Zero Azimuth

Thank you for your interest in contributing to this project! Your contributions are vital to making this resource valuable for everyone. Please follow the guidelines below to ensure your contributions are in line with project goals.

## How to Submit Evidence
1. **Gather Information:** Ensure that your evidence is credible and from a reliable source. Provide context for the evidence you submit, detailing why it is significant.
2. **Format Evidence:** Present the evidence in a clear and concise manner. Use bullet points or tables where necessary for clarity.
3. **Submit Evidence:** Open an issue or a pull request with a detailed description of your evidence. Include any relevant links, documents, or studies that support your submission.

## Verifying Sources
1. **Credibility Check:** Always check the credibility of your sources. Look for reputable publications, official statements, or peer-reviewed articles.
2. **Cross-reference:** Verify facts against multiple sources. Ensure that the information you provide is consistent across different credible platforms.
3. **Cite Sources:** Always cite your sources. Include links or references to the materials used to support your claims.

## Participating in the Project
1. **Respect Community Guidelines:** Be respectful in discussions and contributions. Harassment or disrespectful behavior will not be tolerated.
2. **Join Discussions:** Engage in discussions about the project. Share your thoughts, suggestions, and feedback.
3. **Review Pull Requests:** If you're familiar with the project, consider reviewing pull requests from others. Your insights can greatly help in maintaining quality.
4. **Ask Questions:** If you're unsure about something, don't hesitate to ask questions. The community is here to help you!

Thank you for contributing to Awesome Transnational Criminal Audits by Zero Azimuth. Every contribution helps to make this project better!
