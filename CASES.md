## Documentation Structure

### Sections
1. **Case Study Title** - The title of the case.
2. **Overview** - A brief summary of the case.
3. **Details** - Important details including date, location, type of crime, and key individuals.
4. **Findings** - Summary of the results and insights gained.
5. **Conclusion** - Reflections on the impact and lessons learned.

### Best Practices
- Be clear and concise.
- Use bullet points for clarity.
- Ensure all data is accurate and cited where necessary.

### References
- Include any references used for the case study, such as articles, reports, or texts.
